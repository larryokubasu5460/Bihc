Building character and inspiring careers

A world class Swiss Hotel Management College seeking to educate and influence the next generation of hoteliers.

BIHC, in partnership with the BHMS, is here to nurture your interest in the hospitality industry & empower your ambitions.

There is nothing that can stop you from achieving what you want, except yourself. If you devote yourself to it you will achieve your goal.

Why study at BIHC?

We are renown across Africa for our state of the art study programs

  • Graduate with Swiss-accredited programs in Culinary Arts and Hotel Management in partnership with the Business and Hotel Management School, Lucerne
  • Study and gain practical skills in a 5-Star Hotel environment
  • Study in Kenya - Africa's Leading Destination according to the World Travel Awards 2019
  • Internship programs at leading local and International hotel and restaurant brands as part of your study package
  • Travel the world through our paid International Internships Network; the United States of America, United Arab Emirates, the Caribbean and the Far East
